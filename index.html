Where should I launch my game?



Yes. No seriously... yes. There are so many different places you can launch your games today.






Steam is the best monetization platform that exists for games.






Humble Bundle is a great middle-man platform that can help improve your sales by distributing Steam keys.






Game Jolt is the god of demo sites.






Itch.io has more customizability than any other site but unfortunately lacks in the discoverability department.
